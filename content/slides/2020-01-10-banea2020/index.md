## Controls
- Go to Fullscreen mode to properly visualise the slides by pressing: `F`
- Next: `Right Arrow` or `Space`
- Previous: `Left Arrow`
- Start: `Home`
- Finish: `End`
- Overview: `Esc`
- Zoom: `Alt + Click`
